This is my website, I get to choose[^appearance]. But collected here are some suggestions to help me towards consistency:

* This is not formal writing - I don't need or want CMOS, APA, ABA, etc. I'm mostly writing for "Me/Future Me"[^why] as the audience. So my style, to the extent that there is any, should serve me  - as writer and reader.
