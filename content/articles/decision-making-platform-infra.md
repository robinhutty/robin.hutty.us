As a professional Platform/Infrastructure Engineer and as a Systems Architect, I have often been responsible for making decisions about the target to which the application teams' work will/should be deployed and how the deployment will/should work.

I'm not writing (here/now) about what I picked/would pick for this or that project, this piece is more concerned with identifying what are the categories of options, what factors should affect the decision(s), etc. This piece is about decision making itself.

So one of the decisions that I’m considering is: what should be my default choice for “platform” techs?
* (virtual) machines? Which provisioning tech? Which configuration management tech?
* Containerized? Docker/podman, Kubernetes, other?
* (code) Forge/Collaboration software? GitHub, GitLab, other?
