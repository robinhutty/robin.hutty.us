== About dependencies

- `brew leaves -r`
- `brew deps ...`
- `brew deps --tree --installed [formula]`
- `brew leaves | xargs brew deps --formula --for-each | sed "s/^.*:/$(tput setaf 4)&$(tput sgr0)/"` gives a list of all formulae that are not dependencies of any other formulae (leaves), and for each of them lists all of _their_ dependencies[^so-dep-res].
- `brew uses --installed ...`

[^so-dep-res]: [Source](https://stackoverflow.com/a/55445034)

== Linux

There is homebrew on linux, `linuxbrew`, which works out very nicely for me, despite coming with a few differences/limitations:

- No "casks" (which means more or less the same as "no native/graphical applications")
- By default, linuxbrew is elsewhere:

    ```console
    $ command -v brew
    /home/linuxbrew/.linuxbrew/bin/brew
    $ brew --prefix
    /home/linuxbrew/.linuxbrew
    ```
